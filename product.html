Vinegar Egg

Materials needed: Vinegar, Egg, Container

Description: Place Egg in Vinegar for a couple of days, and watch as the shell melts away!

Growing Gummie

Materials needed: Gummie Bear, Water, Container

Description: Placing a Gummie Friend into a container of water for a day will make him grow! The longer he soaks the bigger he gets!

Painting Flowers

Materials needed: Flowers(White Carnations), Food Coloring, Water, Container

Description: Place your perfectly white flowers into water colored with food coloring, and watch as the colored water travels up the stem and into the petals, turning them colors!

Coke and Mentos

Materials needed: 1 Liter of Coca-Cola, Pack of Mentos

Description: An Explosive Reaction of Suger, placing a Mentos mint into a bottle of Coca-Cola will result in a stream of foam flying out before your eyes!
